Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Virtual Lab AR

How can I implement Virtual Lab AR in my school?

Contact us for a personalized consultation. We'll assess your needs, provide training for teachers, and help you integrate our platform into your existing curriculum.

What devices are compatible with Virtual Lab AR?

Our platform works on smartphones, tablets, and computers with modern web browsers. AR features are available on ARCore and ARKit compatible devices.

Is there a cost to use Virtual Lab AR?

We offer different pricing tiers including free access for qualifying rural schools. Contact us to discuss the best option for your institution.

Do you provide teacher training and support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training programs, ongoing support, and curriculum resources to help teachers effectively integrate AR technology into their science lessons.
